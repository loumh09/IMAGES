Full Description
This augmented reality(AR) allows students see the the solar system in motion. By pointing the camera at the given pictures, you will have the possibility to visualize each planets of our solar system and learn of them like you never did before.Only with your mobile or tablet android phones.
Sol Ar is a best app for viewing the planets and solar system in 3d and helps you to know more about our solar system and our planetys in 3d view and see the distance in the sun and their orbits.
This app is for educational purpose for elementary students who wants to read more about solar system and much more about planets.
This app is offline and no need internet.
